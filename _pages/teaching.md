## Fall 2025
### MATH 281 / ME 201: Applied Boundary Value Problems
Taught by [Prof. Hussein Aluie](https://www.hajim.rochester.edu/me/people/faculty/aluie_hussein/index.html), UR Dept of Mechanical Engineering 
Class size: 52
Level: Advanced undergraduate, introductory graduate
Subject: Partial Differential Equations  
TA duties: Weekly office hours, occasional workshops; graded biweekly homework assignments and midterm/final projects.
